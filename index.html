Frequently Asked Questions

To contact a seller, go to their shop page and click the "Contact Shop Owner" button. You can send them a message with any questions about their products.

Shipping times vary by seller. Most sellers process orders within 1-3 business days, and delivery times depend on the shipping method chosen. You can find estimated delivery dates on each listing page.

Etsy accepts credit/debit cards, Etsy Gift Cards, Etsy Credits, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and some local payment methods depending on your country.

To open a shop, go to Etsy.com and click "Sell on Etsy." You'll need to create an account, choose your shop name, add listings, set up payment methods, and configure your shop policies.

Etsy charges a $0.20 listing fee per item, a 6.5% transaction fee on the sale price (including shipping), and a 3% + $0.25 payment processing fee. There may be additional fees for optional services like Etsy Ads.

First, check the shop's return policy. If returns are accepted, contact the seller to arrange the return. Most sellers will provide return instructions. You may need to pay return shipping unless the item was damaged or not as described.

Etsy's Purchase Protection program covers eligible purchases that never arrive or arrive damaged. You must contact the seller first and attempt to resolve the issue. If unresolved after 48 hours, you can open a case with Etsy.

If the seller provided tracking, you'll find it in your order confirmation email or by going to "Purchases and Reviews" in your Etsy account. Click on the order to view tracking information.

Yes, Etsy allows the sale of digital products like printables, patterns, and downloads. You'll need to set up automatic downloads through Etsy's digital delivery system.

You can change your shop name once by going to Shop Manager > Settings > Info & Appearance. After one change, you'll need to contact Etsy support for additional changes.

If your account is suspended, check your email for a message from Etsy explaining why. You can appeal the suspension by contacting Etsy support and providing any requested information.
